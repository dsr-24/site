DESCRIPTION

Deadpool’s world is in danger, and the Time Variance Authority pulls him into a mission. He teams up with Wolverine from another universe to stop a powerful threat. When they travel through the multiverse, the two clash, fight enemies, and face chaos, trying to save their worlds before it’s too late.
