DESCRIPTION

The Fantastic Four must defend Earth from Galactus, a celestial entity that feeds on planets. Caught between conflict and cosmic power, they face impossible choices to protect humanity and each other. As Marvel's First Family, they'll discover what it truly means to be heroes when the fate of the world hangs in the balance.
